Maven Music Challenge - Top 5 (Out of 152 Participants)

In my first-ever data analytics challenge, the Maven Music Challenge, I secured a spot in the top 5 out of 152 participants.

Project Goal

For the Maven Music Challenge, the objective was to create a personalized Spotify Wrapped experience by analyzing and visualizing streaming history. Participants could either use their own Spotify data or the provided sample dataset—I opted for the sample dataset to craft engaging insights.

Project Approach

To bring this project to life, I combined multiple tools and techniques:

  • 𝗣𝗼𝘄𝗲𝗿 𝗕𝗜 for interactive visualizations
  • 𝗣𝘆𝘁𝗵𝗼𝗻 + 𝗦𝗽𝗼𝘁𝗶𝗳𝘆 𝗔𝗣𝗜 to fetch track images for a richer experience
  • 𝗙𝗶𝗴𝗺𝗮 for a custom background design to enhance the visual appeal
  • 𝗗𝗮𝘁𝗮-𝗱𝗿𝗶𝘃𝗲𝗻 𝘀𝘁𝗼𝗿𝘆𝘁𝗲𝗹𝗹𝗶𝗻𝗴 to capture the essence of the challenge
The result is a compelling data story that mimics the excitement of Spotify Wrapped, transforming raw listening data into an intuitive and visually appealing dashboard. This project was a fun blend of data analysis, visualization, and design, showcasing how we can turn raw data into compelling insights.
