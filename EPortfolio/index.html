Internship

Tomorrowland Data Maestro: unlocking festival magic with data-driven & ML insights

Between the 26th of February, 2024, and the 24th of May, 2024, I did a 13-week internship at EpicData. EpicData, based in Kontich, Belgium, is a leading data consultancy firm formed in 2022 from the merger of AtOnce and DataMotive. Part of the Cronos Group, EpicData helps organizations become data-driven powerhouses, offering tailored solutions in data management, analytics, and visualization. With expertise in technologies like Power BI, SAP Analytics Cloud, and Snowflake, EpicData excels in creating data strategies, building platforms, and implementing BI tools. Their customized approach ensures clients achieve sustainable, data-driven success.

The objective of my internship project was to provide actionable insights into various aspects of the Tomorrowland festival, utilizing Power BI. By leveraging the data visualization and analysis capabilities of Power BI, I aimed to enhance operational efficiency, maximize revenue, and improve the overall attendee experience. This involves not only creating comprehensive visualizations that highlight key trends and insights but also developing data-driven strategies that can be implemented to address specific challenges faced by the festival. Through detailed data analysis and interpretation, the goal is to support Tomorrowland in making informed decisions that lead to a more efficient and enjoyable festival for all involved.
